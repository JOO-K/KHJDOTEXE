Cardio

In the year 2020 I ate a lot of eggs. Fried, boiled, or scrambled, eggs are a great supplement to one's meals. The local market sold a dozen large eggs for around $2.50. Sometimes when I was feeling fancy I would buy the pack with orange yolk for a bit more.

Researchers studied nearly half a million Chinese adults over nine years and found up to one egg per day led to a lower risk of heart disease and stroke. Experts have pointed out, however, that participants in that study were not eating a Western diet.

I made this sketch in honor of eggs and then converted the drawing into a SVG file, which was then brought into blender to be turned into a 3d object.



Audio: Demolition Group- Bad Gag, A song listened to while consuming eggs.
