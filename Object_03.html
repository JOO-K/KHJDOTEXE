Eyes

This is the streetview of the area around where I lived for the past year. Although I had lived in Los Angeles my entire life, it was my first time living alone in the middle of the city. The past few years(decades) rent in LA has gotten a lot higher and the area I once culturally identified with is now gentrified. It was shocking seeing the changes across this metropolitan landscape that impacted life throughout this small ecosystem.

I lived in a small garage retrofitted to be a 1 bedroom apartment. There was no wifi and no kitchen, so I spent a lot of my time listening to music that I would download at the local library.
