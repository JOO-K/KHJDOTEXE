Spine

A person's height is quite important to the forming of the person's perspective on the world. It is the constant that affects our understanding of the relations between other people and things. How differently would we view the world if we were the size of ants? The average human scale of existence imbues people with the dual mentality of dominance and humility. Seemingly miniscule compared to an elephant or a whale, people combine their efforts to form towering structures that are many times the size of any biological creature.

This is a rendering of several objects, structures, and beings compared to my height. Have a look at my perspective of the world.
